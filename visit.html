Address

Pokémon Center MEGA TOKYO & Pikachu Sweets
Sunshine City alpa 2F
3-1-2 Higashi-Ikebukuro
Toshima-ku, Tokyo, 170-6002

Access

The location is near Higashi-Ikebukuro Station and Ikebukuro Station. It is inside a mall-style facility, which makes it easier to reach by train and on foot.

Parking & Accessibility

Visitors can use Sunshine City facility services. Mall facilities typically include elevators, escalators, restrooms, and accessible indoor paths. Check Sunshine City details before a real trip.

Before you go

  • Check the official store page for closures or schedule changes.
  • Bring a deck if you want to play casual battles.
  • Bring sleeves or a binder if you plan to buy cards.
  • Save extra time for Pikachu Sweets and photo spots.
