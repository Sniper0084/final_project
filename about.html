About the location

Pokémon cards, merch, sweets, and battles under one roof

Mega Card Vault is based on Pokémon Center MEGA TOKYO & Pikachu Sweets, a real Pokémon destination inside Sunshine City alpa in Ikebukuro, Tokyo.

History & Background

Pokémon Center MEGA TOKYO is part of the official Pokémon Center store family. This project focuses on the TCG side of the experience by highlighting the Pokémon Card Station: a permanent area where visitors can enjoy Pokémon cards, buy card products, learn rules, and join events.

The location also connects to Pikachu Sweets by Pokémon Cafe, a no-reservation takeout shop, making the area more than just a regular retail store.
